## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to grant any permissions?

**A:** Yes, you need to grant "Display over other apps" permission for the popup to work. The app will ask for this automatically.

### Q: Does it work with old screenshots?

**A:** Yes! You can share any payment screenshot from your Gallery, even old ones.

### Q: What if the amount is wrong?

**A:** The OCR is >90% accurate, but if it's wrong, you can dismiss the popup and add the expense manually.

### Q: Does it work offline?

**A:** Yes! All OCR processing happens on your device. No internet needed.

### Q: Is my data safe?

**A:** Absolutely! All processing is on-device. No screenshots are uploaded or stored.

### Q: Can I share multiple screenshots?

**A:** Currently, only the first image is processed. We'll add batch processing soon!

### Q: What if Money Manager doesn't appear in share menu?

**A:** Make sure the app is installed and updated. Try restarting your phone.
