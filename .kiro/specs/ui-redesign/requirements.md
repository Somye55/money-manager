# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document outlines the requirements for redesigning the Money Manager mobile application UI to achieve a production-grade, modern mobile experience. The redesign will focus on visual polish, improved user experience, and mobile-first design patterns that feel native and professional.

## Glossary

- **Production_Grade_UI**: A user interface that meets professional standards with polished visuals, consistent design patterns, and smooth interactions
- **Mobile_First_Design**: Design approach that prioritizes mobile user experience and interaction patterns
- **Visual_Hierarchy**: Clear organization of content through typography, spacing, and visual weight
- **Design_System**: Consistent set of design tokens, components, and patterns used throughout the application
- **Touch_Optimized**: Interface elements sized and spaced appropriately for finger interaction
- **Native_Feel**: User interface that follows platform conventions and feels like a native mobile app

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a user, I want the app to have a modern, polished visual design, so that it feels professional and trustworthy for managing my finances.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing any screen, THE Visual_Design SHALL use consistent typography, spacing, and color schemes
2. WHEN interacting with elements, THE Visual_Feedback SHALL provide smooth animations and transitions
3. WHEN viewing content, THE Visual_Hierarchy SHALL guide users through information clearly
4. WHEN using the app, THE Design_Consistency SHALL be maintained across all screens and components
5. WHEN comparing to other apps, THE Visual_Quality SHALL meet modern mobile app standards

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want the interface to feel native and responsive, so that interactions are smooth and intuitive.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN tapping buttons, THE Touch_Response SHALL provide immediate visual feedback
2. WHEN scrolling content, THE Scroll_Behavior SHALL be smooth with appropriate momentum
3. WHEN navigating between screens, THE Transitions SHALL be fluid and purposeful
4. WHEN interacting with forms, THE Input_Experience SHALL feel native and responsive
5. WHEN using gestures, THE Gesture_Recognition SHALL respond appropriately to swipes and taps

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a user, I want the dashboard to present financial information clearly and attractively, so that I can quickly understand my financial status.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing the dashboard, THE Information_Layout SHALL prioritize the most important financial data
2. WHEN displaying numbers, THE Data_Visualization SHALL be clear and easy to read
3. WHEN showing categories, THE Visual_Representation SHALL use appropriate colors and icons
4. WHEN presenting charts, THE Chart_Design SHALL be clean and informative
5. WHEN viewing balances, THE Financial_Data SHALL be prominently displayed with clear formatting

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a user, I want consistent and attractive form interfaces, so that adding expenses and managing data is pleasant and efficient.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN filling forms, THE Form_Design SHALL be clean with clear field labels and validation
2. WHEN entering data, THE Input_Fields SHALL be appropriately sized and styled
3. WHEN encountering errors, THE Error_States SHALL be clearly communicated with helpful messaging
4. WHEN submitting forms, THE Loading_States SHALL provide clear feedback
5. WHEN using dropdowns and selectors, THE Selection_Interface SHALL be intuitive and touch-friendly

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a user, I want the navigation to be intuitive and visually appealing, so that moving through the app is effortless.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN using bottom navigation, THE Navigation_Design SHALL be clean with clear icons and labels
2. WHEN viewing the current page, THE Active_State SHALL be clearly indicated
3. WHEN tapping navigation items, THE State_Changes SHALL be immediate and smooth
4. WHEN navigating, THE Visual_Consistency SHALL be maintained across all navigation elements
5. WHEN using the app, THE Navigation_Accessibility SHALL support easy thumb navigation

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a user, I want the app to have proper spacing, typography, and layout, so that content is easy to read and visually pleasing.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN reading text, THE Typography SHALL be clear with appropriate font sizes and line heights
2. WHEN viewing content, THE Spacing SHALL create clear visual separation between elements
3. WHEN using the app, THE Layout SHALL be consistent and well-organized
4. WHEN viewing on different screen sizes, THE Responsive_Design SHALL adapt appropriately
5. WHEN scanning content, THE Content_Hierarchy SHALL guide attention effectively

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As a user, I want attractive and functional card-based layouts, so that information is organized and easy to digest.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing cards, THE Card_Design SHALL have appropriate shadows, borders, and spacing
2. WHEN displaying content in cards, THE Content_Organization SHALL be logical and scannable
3. WHEN interacting with cards, THE Interactive_States SHALL provide appropriate feedback
4. WHEN viewing multiple cards, THE Card_Consistency SHALL be maintained across all instances
5. WHEN using cards for data, THE Information_Density SHALL balance detail with readability

### Requirement 8

**User Story:** As a user, I want the color scheme and theming to be modern and professional, so that the app feels current and trustworthy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN using the app, THE Color_Palette SHALL be modern and professionally chosen
2. WHEN switching themes, THE Theme_Consistency SHALL be maintained across all components
3. WHEN viewing different content types, THE Color_Usage SHALL be purposeful and meaningful
4. WHEN comparing to other apps, THE Visual_Style SHALL feel contemporary and polished
5. WHEN using accessibility features, THE Color_Contrast SHALL meet accessibility standards
